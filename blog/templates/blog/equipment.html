Sadly I can't do a bicycle tour without a bike or equipment to go with it. What follows is a list of things I'm bringing for this trip.

As I had some trouble actually finding out what things cost, for some reason nobody wants to put this number online, I want to give future tourers this information!

I'm also going to state the price for all the items. Prices will be in American dollars (even though I bought a lot of things in Sweden, where things are a great deal more expensive than the US).

So don't see this as the definite cost to bike touring, but as a general rule of thumb. Also I should mention again that I'm not sponsored in any way.
