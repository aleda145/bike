Why are you doing this!?

Short answer: Because I can.

Long answer: I want to see the world. Everything. Every river, every city, every mountain. And the best way to see everything is on a bicycle. I did a short tour in southern Germany, Austria and Slovakia in the summer of 2015. I loved every single second of it. I passed a nudist beach at the Danube, ate free cucumbers at a shed set up by a farmer with bent cucumbers that just said "GURKEN", had cold fresh locally produced milk from a vending machine somewhere in Bavaria. And thats just some highlights from a week.

Imagine what I'd experience on this trip!

About me

So you probably pressed the about me page to actually know about me, so here we go:

I grew up in rural Sweden, back then I loathed nature. It was just everywhere around me and absolutely super boring. But then I got older, went to university to study engineering. Did three years, one in Germany and got two years left on my degree. Rationally I should finish my degree before doing anything else but I just can't wait. To finance this trip I've been working as a science teacher from October 2015-June 2016 and well yeah, that's been an experience. Working has just made me even more hyped for the trip!
