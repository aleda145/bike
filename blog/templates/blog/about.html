Hey.


That's me. In a tree.

Why are you doing this!?

Short answer: Because I can.

Long answer: I want to see the world. Everything. Every river, every city, every mountain. And the best way to see everything is on a bicycle. I did a short tour in southern Germany, Austria and Slovakia in the summer of 2015. I loved every single second of it. I passed a nudist beach at the Danube, ate free cucumbers at a shed set up by a farmer with bent cucumbers that just said "GURKEN", had cold fresh locally produced milk from a vending machine somewhere in Bavaria. And thats just some highlights from a week.

Imagine what I'd experience in 50 weeks!

About me

So you probably pressed the about me page to actually know about me, so here we go:

I grew up in rural Sweden, back then I loathed nature. It was just everywhere around me and absolutely super boring. But then I got older. Now I love the outdoors and want see all the magnificent things Mother Earth has created!

After high school I went to university to study engineering. Did three years, one in Germany and got two years left on my degree. Rationally I should finish my degree before doing anything else but I just can't wait. When is the next time in my life where I will have so much spare time as I do know? The answer is probably retirement, and that is simply too far away.

To finance this trip I've been working as a science teacher from October 2015-June 2016. It's been very rewarding but also very stressful at times. It was a cool part of my life, but I think I would never want to be a teacher, but hey, you gotta try out everything! I also know some of my past students are probably reading this (hej!), sorry for making you make so many lab reports!

I'll probably add some more things here later, in the meantime, check out my blog!
